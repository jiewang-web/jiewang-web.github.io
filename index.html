I am a Ph.D. candidate at Washington University in St. Louis (WashU) in the Electrical & Systems Engineering (ESE) Department. I am a member of the Sensing and Processing Across Networks (SPAN) Lab led by Professor Neal Patwari. Prior to WashU, I received a B.S. degree in Electronics and Information Engineering and a B.A. degree in English from Sichuan University in 2019.

My research interests include machine learning for wireless, dynamic spectrum sharing, wireless sensor networking, Open RAN, and RF sensing. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in our research!
